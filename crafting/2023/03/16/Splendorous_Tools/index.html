    Introduction

    Splendorous Tools are the level 90+ relic tools for Disciples of the Hand and Land, introduced in patch 6.35 and expanded through Dawntrail. This comprehensive guide covers all six upgrade stages from base Splendorous Tools to the final Lodestar Tools.

    What You’ll Learn:

    • Unlock requirements and prerequisites
    • All 6 upgrade stages with materials and costs
    • Efficient scrip farming strategies
    • Crafting macros for each stage
    • Gathering collectables and locations

    Total Time Investment: 15-30 hours per job (varies by playstyle)
    Total Scrip Cost: ~4,000-6,000 Purple Scrips per job

    Note: Purple Scrips are officially called “Purple Crafter’s Scrips” (for DoH) and “Purple Gatherer’s Scrips” (for DoL). They appear purple in-game despite sometimes being referred to as “White Scrips” in older content.

    Quick Reference: All Tool Stages

    Stage Tool Name Requirements Scrip Cost
    0 Splendorous Tool Quest reward (first free) 1,500 per job
    1 Augmented Splendorous 20 perfect crafts (60 components) ~1,000
    2 Crystalline Tool 30 perfect crafts (90 components) ~1,500
    3 Chora-Zoi’s Crystalline 30 perfect crafts (90 components) ~1,500
    4 Brilliant Tool 30 perfect crafts (90 components) ~2,000
    5 Vrandtic Visionary’s 20 crafts (60 components) ~1,500
    6 Lodestar Tool (Final) 20 crafts (60 components) ~2,000

    Prerequisites & Unlock

    Requirements

    Before starting, you must have:

    • Level 90+ Disciple of the Hand or Land
    • Completed Endwalker Main Scenario Quest
    • Unlocked The Crystalline Mean (Crystarium feature quest)
    • Completed “The Boutique Always Wins” quest

    Unlock Quest

    Quest: An Original Improvement
    NPC: Chora-Zoi
    Location: The Crystarium (X:7.8, Y:11.4)

    Reward: Splendorous Coffer (contains tool for your current job)

    Stage 0: Acquiring Base Splendorous Tools

    First Tool (Free)

    Complete “An Original Improvement” to receive your first Splendorous Coffer. Open it on the job you want the tool for.

    Additional Tools (1,500 Purple Scrips Each)

    Purchase from Quinnana in The Crystarium (X:10.4, Y:7.7).

    Available Tools:

    • Carpenter: Splendorous Saw
    • Blacksmith: Splendorous Cross-pein Hammer
    • Armorer: Splendorous Raising Hammer
    • Goldsmith: Splendorous Chaser Hammer
    • Leatherworker: Splendorous Head Knife
    • Weaver: Splendorous Needle
    • Alchemist: Splendorous Alembic
    • Culinarian: Splendorous Frypan
    • Miner: Splendorous Pickaxe
    • Botanist: Splendorous Hatchet
    • Fisher: Splendorous Fishing Rod

    Budget Planning: If doing all 11 jobs, you’ll need 15,000 Purple Scrips just for base tools (first is free).

    Stage 1: Augmented Splendorous Tools

    Quest Details

    Quest: A Dedicated Tool (Repeatable)
    NPC: Chora-Zoi
    Location: The Crystarium (X:10.5, Y:7.7)
    Requirement: Splendorous Tool equipped or in inventory

    Crafters: Materials & Strategy

    Objective: Craft 20 collectables with max collectability

    Turn-in: 60 Splendorous Components to Quinnana

    Materials per Craft:

    • 1x Select Material (50 Purple Scrips from Quinnana)
    • 2x Gatherable Material

    Total Cost: ~1,000 Purple Scrips per job.

    Pro Tip: Use Rarefied Tsai tou Vonou or similar HQ food to boost stats. The Trained Eye ability guarantees max collectability instantly.

    Crafter Recipes by Job

    Carpenter: Connoisseur’s Chair

    • 1x Select Ironwood Lumber (50 scrips)
    • 2x Ironwood Log

    Blacksmith: Connoisseur’s Wall Lantern

    • 1x Select Manganese Ingot (50 scrips)
    • 2x Phrygian Gold Ore

    Armorer: Connoisseur’s Ornate Door

    • 1x Select Titanium Plate (50 scrips)
    • 2x Ironwood Log

    Goldsmith: Connoisseur’s Vanity Mirror

    • 1x Select Crystal Glass (50 scrips)
    • 2x Bismuth Ore

    Leatherworker: Connoisseur’s Rug

    • 1x Select Smilodon Leather (50 scrips)
    • 2x Almasty Fur

    Weaver: Connoisseur’s Swag Valance

    • 1x Select Scarlet Moko Cloth (50 scrips)
    • 2x Almasty Fur

    Alchemist: Connoisseur’s Cosmetics Box

    • 1x Select Hoptrap Leaf (50 scrips)
    • 2x Petalouda Scales

    Culinarian: Connoisseur’s Pixieberry Tea

    • 1x Select Pixieberry (50 scrips)
    • 2x Sideritis Leaves

    Gatherers: Collectables

    Miner: Connoisseur’s Primstone

    • Quantity: 180 total
    • Location: Check Gathering Log (Sidequest section)

    Botanist: Connoisseur’s Wattle Petribark

    • Quantity: 180 total
    • Location: Check Gathering Log (Sidequest section)

    Fisher: Clavekeeper or Platinum Seahorse

    • Quantity: 120 fish total
    • Bait: Select Bait Ball (5 Purple Scrips from Quinnana)
    • CRITICAL: Turn on Collectables mode before casting
    • Use Patience II and Cordials for efficiency

    Stage 2: Crystalline Tools

    Quest Details

    Quest: An Adaptive Tool (Repeatable)
    NPC: Chora-Zoi
    Location: The Crystarium (X:10.5, Y:7.7)
    Requirement: Augmented Splendorous Tool in inventory

    Crafters: Materials & Strategy

    Objective: Craft 30 collectables with max collectability

    Turn-in: 90 Adaptive Components to Quinnana

    Total Cost: ~1,500 Purple Scrips per job

    Note: Recipes require 3-4 materials including Select items. Continue using Trained Eye for instant max collectability.

    Example: Carpenter Recipe

    Connoisseur’s Marimba:

    • 1x Select Ironwood Lumber (50 scrips)
    • 1x Integral Lumber
    • 1x Chondrite Ingot

    Gatherers: Collectables

    Miner: Connoisseur’s Red Malachite

    • Quantity: 180 total (570+ collectability)

    Botanist: Connoisseur’s Levin Mint

    • Quantity: 180 total (570+ collectability)

    Fisher: Spangled Pirarucu or Mirror Image

    • Quantity: 120 fish
    • Bait: Select Bait Ball (5 scrips each)
    • Target collectability: 425+ for Spangled Pirarucu

    Stage 3: Chora-Zoi’s Crystalline Tools

    Quest Details

    Quest: A Tool of Her Own (Repeatable)
    NPC: Chora-Zoi (speak to Grenoldt first to complete “The Joy of Zoi”)
    Requirement: Crystalline Tool in inventory

    Crafters

    Objective: 30 max collectability crafts
    Turn-in: 90 Customized Components

    Materials: 3-4 ingredients including Select items
    Cost: ~1,500 Purple Scrips per job

    Stage 4: Brilliant Tools

    Quest Details

    Quest: A Tool without Compare (Repeatable)
    NPC: Chora-Zoi
    Requirement: Chora-Zoi’s Crystalline Tool in inventory

    Crafters

    Objective: 30 max collectability crafts
    Turn-in: 90 Brilliant Components

    Materials: 4 ingredients including Select items
    Cost: ~2,000 Purple Scrips per job

    Example: Carpenter (Connoisseur’s Bookshelf):

    • 1x Select Dark Chestnut Lumber
    • 1x Integral Lumber
    • 1x Select Ironwood Lumber
    • 1x Manganese Ingot

    Efficient Purple Scrip Farming

    Custom Deliveries (Best Passive Method)

    Weekly Reset: Tuesday at 8 AM server time

    NPCs: M’naago, Adkiragh, Kai-Shirr, Kurenai, Ameliance, etc.

    Scrips per Week: 1,500-2,000 (varies by satisfaction bonus)

    Time Investment: 30-60 minutes per week

    Strategy: This alone can complete 1-2 relic tools per week with zero additional grinding.

    Collectable Turn-Ins (Active Farming)

    Top Tier Crafting Collectables

    1. Rarefied Giant Popoto Pancakes (Culinarian)

    • Materials: 4 main ingredients + 2 pre-crafts
    • Difficulty: Easy (cruise control)
    • Strategy: Quick synth base materials, macro the final craft

    2. Rarefied Integral Armillae (Carpenter)

    • Materials: Moderate (some Weaver materials needed)
    • Difficulty: Easy
    • Note: Crystal-intensive

    3. Rarefied Draught (Alchemist)

    • Materials: Berkanan Sap (farmable via FATEs)
    • Difficulty: Easy
    • Strategy: Trade Bicolor Gemstones to Faezbroez in Labyrinthos for Berkanan Sap

    Gathering Collectables

    Any level 90+ collectable works. Check your Gathering Log for current options and choose based on:

    • Node location convenience
    • Material value (sell extras on market board)
    • Collectability threshold difficulty

    Cost Breakdown: Complete Tool Progression

    Per Job Cost (Scrips)

    Stage Crafter Cost Gatherer Cost
    Base Tool 1,500 1,500
    Augmented 1,000 ~600 (bait)
    Crystalline 1,500 ~600 (bait)
    Chora-Zoi’s 1,500 ~600 (bait)
    Brilliant 2,000 ~800 (bait)
    Vrandtic Visionary’s 1,500 ~800 (bait)
    Lodestar 2,000 ~1,000 (bait)
    TOTAL ~11,000 ~6,900

    Additional Costs

    Food: Mole Loaf (HQ) or Rarefied Tsai tou Vonou (HQ)

    • Cost: 5,000-20,000 gil per stack
    • Usage: Stages 5-6 require food buffs

    Tinctures: Draught (HQ)

    • Cost: 10,000-30,000 gil per stack
    • Usage: Stage 6 (final) requires tincture

    Tips & Strategies

    Efficient Progression Path

    Week 1: Complete Custom Deliveries, buy base tool
    Week 2: Grind Augmented stage (Trained Eye spam)
    Week 3: Complete Crystalline stage
    Week 4: Complete Chora-Zoi’s stage
    Week 5: Complete Brilliant stage
    Week 6-7: Complete final two stages (require macros)

    Total Time: 6-8 weeks casually, 1-2 weeks if grinding hard

    Multi-Job Strategy

    Parallel Progression:

    • Buy all 8 crafter base tools at once (10,500 scrips)
    • Progress all crafters through Stage 1-4 together (Trained Eye spam)
    • Stages 5-6: One job at a time (macro requirements)

    Benefits:

    • Efficient scrip usage
    • Batch crafting materials
    • Faster overall completion

    Gatherer-Specific Tips

    Fishing:

    • Use Patience II for double components on high collectability
    • Hi-Cordial or Cordial to recover GP
    • Food buff helps meet collectability thresholds

    Mining/Botanist:

    • Use Collector’s Glove rotation
    • Gather extra for market board sales
    • Hit 570+ collectability for double components

    Troubleshooting

    “I can’t hit max collectability”

    Stage 1-4: Use Trained Eye (instant max collectability)

    Stage 5-6:

    • Verify your stats meet macro requirements
    • Use HQ food and tincture (stage 6)
    • Overmeld if necessary
    • Consider melding Cunning materia for CP

    “Macros aren’t working”

    Common Issues:

    • Lag causing actions to fail (increase wait times in the macro)
    • Stats too low (verify min requirements)
    • Not using food/tincture
    • Wrong tool equipped

    “Gathering collectables is slow”

    Solutions:

    • Use GP rotation guides (Diadem skills)
    • Equip better gathering gear
    • Use food for stat boost
    • Cordials for GP recovery

    Achievement Rewards

    Completing all Lodestar Tools grants:

    • “Lodestar Luminary” title (all 11 tools completed)
    • Glamour options for all tool stages
    • Satisfaction of completing endgame relic grind
