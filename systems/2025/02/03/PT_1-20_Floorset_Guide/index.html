    Deep Dungeon PT 1-20 — Floorset Guide

    This guide covers Floors 1–20 of Pilgrims Traverse, split into two difficulty bands. Early floors introduce mechanics safely, while Floors 11–20 escalate damage, enemy density, and mimic risk.

    PT 1-10 Overview

    Floors Rooms Enemies Chests Mimics Hoard Reward
    1-10 3-6 2-3 4-6 Bronze chests on 7-10 Sack of Silvered Light

    Respawn Timer: 1 minute

    Key Notes

    • Floors 1-6 have no bronze chests, therefore no mimics.
    • Floors 1-6 do not drop:
      • Alteration
      • Purity
      • Serenity
      • Purification
      • Raising
      • Devotion
    • Silver chests can drop incense starting on Floor 1.
    • Pomanders of Affluence, Flight, and Alteration do not drop on Floor 9.
    • Bronze chests in this set do not drop potsherds.

    Early floors can hurt through overlapping AoEs but rarely kill outright. Mimic Deathtrap is the only lethal mechanic introduced here and only appears later in the set.

    Most enemies here are vulnerable to bind, heavy, sleep, slow, and stun.

    PT 11-20 Overview

    Floors Rooms Enemies Chests Mimics Hoard Reward
    11-20 3-6 2-3 4-6 Bronze chests 11-19 Sack of Silvered Light

    Respawn Timer: 1 minute

    Key Notes

    • Mimics can appear from bronze chests on Floors 11-19.
    • Floors 11-13 do not drop potsherds from bronze chests.
    • Pomanders of Affluence, Flight, and Alteration do not drop on Floor 19.
    • Damage ramps significantly compared to PT 1-10.

    This floorset introduces more patrols, sound-based agro, tighter room spacing, and higher punishment for mistakes.

    Forgiven Disobedience (Dzo) 16-17 Sight Line charge
    Forgiven Complacency 16-19 Sight Shadow AoE
    Forgiven Contempt (Patrol) 17-19 Sight Fire breath
    Forgiven Conceit 18-19 Sight Heavy conal
    Forgiven Violence 18-19 Sight High damage AoE
    Mimic 11-19 Proximity Deathtrap on chest open

    Enemy telegraphs are faster and overlap more frequently than PT 1-10.

    Strategy Summary

    PT 1-10

    • Learn enemy tells and spacing.
    • Open chests freely on Floors 1-6.
    • Save mitigation for Floors 7-10 if mimics appear.

    PT 11-20

    • Treat every bronze chest as dangerous.
    • Track patrol paths before pulling.
    • Save utility pomanders ahead of Floor 19.
    • Interrupt or stun aggressively to reduce damage spikes.
