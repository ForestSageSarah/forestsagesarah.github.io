## Introduction

Splendorous Gathering Tools are the level 90+ relic tools for Disciples of the Land (Miner, Botanist, Fisher), introduced in patch 6.35 and expanded through Dawntrail. This comprehensive guide covers all six upgrade stages from base Splendorous Tools to the final Lodestar Tools.

**What You'll Learn:**
- Unlock requirements and prerequisites
- All 6 upgrade stages with locations and collectables
- Efficient gathering rotations for max collectability
- Fishing strategies and bait requirements
- Aetherial Reduction mechanics (Stages 3-6)

**Total Time Investment:** 10-20 hours per job (faster than crafters)  
**Total Scrip Cost:** ~2,000-3,000 Purple Scrips per job

> **Note:** Purple Scrips are officially called "Purple Gatherer's Scrips" for Disciples of the Land. They appear purple in-game despite sometimes being referred to as "White Scrips" in older content.

## Prerequisites & Unlock

### Requirements

Before starting, you must have:
- Level 90+ Disciple of the Land (Miner, Botanist, or Fisher)
- Completed Endwalker Main Scenario Quest
- Unlocked The Crystalline Mean (Crystarium feature quest)
- Unlocked Collectables system

### Unlock Quest

**Quest:** An Original Improvement  
**NPC:** Chora-Zoi  
**Location:** The Crystarium (X:7.8, Y:11.4)

**Reward:** Splendorous Coffer (contains tool for your current job)

---

## Stage 0: Acquiring Base Splendorous Tools

### First Tool (Free)

Complete "An Original Improvement" to receive your first Splendorous Coffer. Open it on the gathering job you want the tool for.

### Additional Tools (1,500 Purple Scrips Each)

Purchase from **Quinnana** in The Crystarium (X:10.4, Y:7.7).

**Available Tools:**
- Miner: Splendorous Pickaxe
- Botanist: Splendorous Hatchet
- Fisher: Splendorous Fishing Rod

**Budget Planning:** If doing all 3 gathering jobs, you'll need 3,000 Purple Scrips for base tools (first is free).

## Gathering Rotations (MIN/BTN)

### Max Collectability Rotation (400 GP)

**Use this rotation when you have 400+ GP:**

```
Scrutiny → Meticulous Prospector → Scrutiny → 
Meticulous Prospector → Meticulous Prospector → Collect
```

**Result:** 1,000 collectability guaranteed

---

### GP Recovery Rotation (Less than 400 GP)

**Use this when waiting for GP to regenerate:**

```
Brazen Prospector → Brazen Prospector → Brazen Prospector → Collect
```

**Result:** Lower collectability, but keeps you gathering

---

### Hidden Items Strategy

**All stages have hidden items (crystals/clusters):**
- Miner: Use Luck of the Mountaineer
- Botanist: Use Luck of the Pioneer

**Pro Tip:** Gather hidden items whenever they appear to conserve GP. They count toward your component total without requiring collectability rotations.

## Fishing Strategies

### Required Item

**Select Bait Ball** - Purchase from Quinnana for **5 Purple Scrips each**

**How many to buy:**
- Stage 1: ~25 bait (120 fish needed)
- Stage 2: ~35 bait (160 fish needed)
- Stages 3-6: ~35 bait each (160-170 fish per stage)

**Total for all 6 stages:** ~200 bait = 1,000 Purple Scrips

### Fishing Rotation for Efficiency

**Before casting:** Enable Collector's Glove (Collect ability)

**Optimal fishing combo:**
```
Identical Cast → Double Hook → Thaliak's Favor
```

**With Cordials:** Use Hi-Cordial between casts to maintain GP

**Result:** Doubles your catch rate, reducing time significantly

**Stages 3-6 use Aetherial Reduction instead of direct turn-ins.**

## Efficient Purple Scrip Farming (Gatherers)

### Custom Deliveries (Best Passive Method)

**Weekly Reset:** Tuesday at 8 AM server time

**NPCs:** M'naago, Adkiragh, Kai-Shirr, Kurenai, Ameliance, etc.

**Scrips per Week:** 1,500-2,000 (varies by satisfaction bonus)

**Time Investment:** 30-60 minutes per week

**Strategy:** Custom Deliveries alone can complete a gathering relic every 2-3 weeks with zero grinding.

### Collectable Gathering (Active Farming)

**Any level 90+ collectable** works. Choose based on:
- Node location convenience
- Short respawn timers
- Easy rotation requirements

**Top Gathering Spots:**
- Labyrinthos nodes (central, clustered)
- Elpis nodes (flying mount friendly)
- Ultima Thule nodes (high-level items)

## Tips & Strategies

### Efficient Progression Path (Miner/Botanist)

**Week 1:** Custom Deliveries, buy base tool  
**Week 2:** Complete Stages 1-2 (direct turn-ins)  
**Week 3:** Complete Stages 3-4 (aetherial reduction)  
**Week 4:** Complete Stages 5-6 (higher volume reduction)

**Total Time:** 4 weeks casually, 1 week if grinding

### Fisher Optimization

**Buy bait in bulk:** Purchase 200 Select Bait Balls at start (1,000 scrips)

**Use GP efficiently:**
- Identical Cast → Double Hook combo
- Thaliak's Favor for rare fish
- Hi-Cordials between casts

**Fish multiple locations:** Alternate between rod/reel component fish to prevent burnout

### Multi-Job Strategy

**Do all 3 gathering jobs together:**
- Buy all base tools (3,000 scrips)
- Progress through stages simultaneously
- Share food and cordials across jobs

**Benefits:**
- Efficient scrip usage
- Variety prevents burnout
- Faster overall completion

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't hit max collectability (1,000)"

**Solutions:**
- Use <span data-xivapi-item="36106">HQ food</span> for stat boost
- Verify you have level 90 left-side gathering gear
- Use the 400 GP rotation (not the recovery rotation)
- Meld gathering stat materia if needed

### "Aetherial Reduction only gives 3 components"

**This is RNG:**
- 3-6 components per reduction is normal
- Average is ~4 components
- Plan for ~55-95 items to get required components
- Hidden items don't require reduction

### "Fishing is taking forever"

**Speed improvements:**
- Always use Identical Cast → Double Hook
- Use Thaliak's Favor for stubborn fish
- Keep Hi-Cordials stocked (recover GP faster)
- Food buff helps catch rate

### "I can't find the gathering node"

**Check:**
- Correct expansion zone (Shadowbringers zones)
- Exact coordinates (not approximate)
- Time of day (some nodes are time-gated)
- Flying unlocked in zone
